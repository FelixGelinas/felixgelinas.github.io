Bonjour-Hi

I am a second-year mathematics PhD student at York University under the supervision of Nantel Bergeron and Vincent Pilaud.

I earned my master’s degree from the University of Toronto in 2023 and my bachelor’s degree from the Université du Québec à Montréal in 2022.

My research lies in combinatorics, focusing on the interplay between partially ordered set theory (posets), polytopes, and hypergraphs.

Enjoy discussing topics in algebraic and enumerative combinatorics and I am always open to connecting with others working on related projects or exploring new ideas in these areas.
